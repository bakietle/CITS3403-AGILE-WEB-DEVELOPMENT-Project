Synopsis

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief who steals valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. His rare ability has made him a coveted player in the world of corporate espionage, but it has also turned him into a fugitive who has lost almost everything that matters to him.

Cobb is offered one last chance at redemption if he can pull off the impossible: inception. Instead of stealing an idea, he and his team must plant one. What starts as a high-stakes mission becomes an emotional journey through layered dreams, memory, and guilt.
